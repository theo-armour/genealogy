# https://geni.com

## About Geni

* Describes itself a social network.
* Enables searching in other users' trees.
* Connects trees made by different users.
* Collaborative editing of friends' trees.

## Geni

* https://www.geni.com/

* <https://www.geni.com/people/Theo-Armour/>

### https://MyHeritage.com

* Owns Geni.com.
* Multilingual user interface.
* Connecting trees made by different users by suggested matches

## Getting started

* Log into both Geni and MyHeritage
* Start in Geni
* On "Welcome" page, in menu bar, click on "Tree"

### Tree View 1

* In Tree view click on any green tree icon at top right of any profile
* Pop up displays number of relatives
* Click on trees with very large numbers
* Clicking will take you to their tree
* Keep going back as far as you want
* Stays in Geni

### Tree View 2

* In Tree view, hover mouse on any magnifying glass
* Click on the one of the boxes
* You will be taken to My Heritage where you can confirm or reject the match
* In MyHeritage you can confirm the link back to the Geni entry
