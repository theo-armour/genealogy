## 2026-07-09 ~ Comments on "GiGi"

My great uncle Sergei was referred to by his family as Gigi.

As far as I can remember, I never met him, and he was rarely mentioned.

I do remember hearing that during WWII he was a Nazi sympathizer. He was exiled as a result. Also that he was a sympathizer because of the forlorn hope that the Nazis might restore the monarchy in Russia.

I also remember hearing that Granny set him a remittance every month.

He went to live in Barcelona and was married to a Spanish woman, Maria del Pilar Robert.

I have been able to find very little about him. Apparently, he went before a French magistrate in 1947 because of some kind of illegal car and car accessory import issues.

Just now (see links above) I see that in 1948 he was sentenced to eighteen months in prison. Could the reason he moved to Spain be that he went there to escape the jail sentence?

***

Eric Van der Graaf reports that Gigi was part owner of his father Prince Sergei's Hotel at 2, rue Montaigne.
