## Geni Tips

As you get further out, the green icon on the right takes a long time to display the number of people in that branch. I find that if I refresh the page, the numbers may appear a little bit more quickly.

If you zoomed out and click on a name, the profile appears in a side boxWhich I don't find terribly convenient. If you zoom up close, the person's name is underlined when you mouseover and it's a link that opens up in a new tab - Which I find convenient.

Horizontal view is great for ancestors! Vertical view is great for partners and siblings.

Geni does a really good job of helping you with Russian names. If you go to the Russian tab in a profile, it will often give the English translation. I'm starting to translate some of the names.
